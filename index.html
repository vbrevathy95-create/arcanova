About ArcaNova

Precision intelligence
for every grow cycle

ArcaNova's Plant Intelligence System transforms how controlled-environment agriculture (CEA) farms operate. Our multi-modal deep learning platform continuously monitors every plant, forecasts yield, detects nutrient deficiencies, and drives automated environment control — all in under 15 milliseconds.

Founded in Paris to close the gap between precision agriculture research and deployable farm technology, ArcaNova brings PhD-level plant science into a production-ready SaaS platform that connects directly to your sensors, actuators, and edge hardware.

Our flagship crop is Wasabi (Wasabia japonica) — one of the most demanding and valuable crops for precision cultivation — but our platform is fully method-agnostic, supporting hydroponic, aeroponic, and soil-based systems across any specialty crop.

Technology

Three streams.
One fusion model.

FusionPlantModel simultaneously processes visual, environmental, and phenological signals — catching stress before it is visible to the human eye.

01 · Vision
Vision
Encoder
EfficientNet-B4 → (B, 512)
Processes 380×380 RGB frames from inside the grow chamber. EfficientNet-B4 backbone pretrained via Noisy Student, projected to 512 dimensions. Detects leaf colour, lesions, morphology, and canopy density.
02 · Sensor
Temporal
Encoder
Transformer ×4 → (B, 256)
Encodes 72 hours of 8-channel sensor data at 15-minute intervals (288 timesteps) via a 4-layer pre-norm Transformer. Captures diurnal cycles, slow nutrient drift, and early stress before symptoms appear visually.
03 · Stage
Stage
Embedding
Embedding(8, 64) · LayerNorm
Conditions all predictions on the current growth stage — germination through post-harvest. Stage-awareness calibrates health thresholds, setpoints, and yield targets to the plant's developmental phase.

SaaS Platform

Everything your farm
needs in one place

API-first, multi-tenant architecture with real-time telemetry, automated alerting, and full fleet management.

01
Real-Time Monitoring
WebSocket telemetry every 30 seconds. Temperature, humidity, pH, EC, PAR, CO₂, and dissolved oxygen — all live, per container.
02
Automated Control
Closed-loop PID control for all environment variables. Stage-aware setpoints advance automatically as the plant develops.
03
Intelligent Alerts
Multi-channel notifications via SMS, email, and push. Custom rules per container, per stage. Severity scoring eliminates alert fatigue.
04
Fleet Management
Manage hundreds of containers from a single tenant view. Geographic overview, health summary, and OTA firmware updates.
05
Yield Analytics
30-day yield forecasting per container with confidence intervals. Historical trends, COGS tracking, and harvest planning export.
06
Open API
Full OpenAPI 3.1 surface. Integrate with ERP, POS, and any third-party system. WebSocket and webhook support included.
